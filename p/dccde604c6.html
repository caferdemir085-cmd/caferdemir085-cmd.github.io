Why Köprüköy Is a Must-Visit for Byzantine Churches Lovers

If someone told me a year ago that Köprüköy would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

What makes Köprüköy special when it comes to Byzantine churches is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The Byzantine churches of Köprüköy reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Köprüköy offers a more intimate and genuine Byzantine churches experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

As I left Köprüköy, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
