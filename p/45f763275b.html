The Hidden World of Caravanserais in Taşova

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Taşova is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

I spoke with several locals about caravanserais in Taşova, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. this is the kind of stuff that restores my faith in the internet.

The infrastructure around caravanserais in Taşova is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

What makes Taşova special when it comes to caravanserais is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

Taşova reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
