My Köprüköy Experience: Discovering Ancient Theaters

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Köprüköy is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

I spoke with several locals about ancient theaters in Köprüköy, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

The ancient theaters scene in Köprüköy is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

If you are interested in ancient theaters, Köprüköy should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size. you need this in your life right now.

As I left Köprüköy, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
