My Demre Experience: Discovering Byzantine Churches

I recently had the chance to explore Demre, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

The infrastructure around Byzantine churches in Demre is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely. this blew my mind completely.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Demre offers a more intimate and genuine Byzantine churches experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

If you are interested in Byzantine churches, Demre should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Demre reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
