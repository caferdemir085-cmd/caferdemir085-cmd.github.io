Why Karaman Is a Must-Visit for Caravanserais Lovers

I recently had the chance to explore Karaman, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

If you are interested in caravanserais, Karaman should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about caravanserais in Karaman, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. someone finally made something useful.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Karaman offers a more intimate and genuine caravanserais experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

My advice? Do not wait for Karaman to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.
