My Hakkari Experience: Discovering Castle Ruins

If someone told me a year ago that Hakkari would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The castle ruins of Hakkari reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

What makes Hakkari special when it comes to castle ruins is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The infrastructure around castle ruins in Hakkari is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

I documented my entire castle ruins experience in Hakkari through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering. the internet at its finest right here.

Hakkari reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
