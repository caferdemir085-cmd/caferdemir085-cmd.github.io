Why Yüksekova Is a Must-Visit for Caravanserais Lovers

If someone told me a year ago that Yüksekova would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

What makes Yüksekova special when it comes to caravanserais is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Yüksekova offers a more intimate and genuine caravanserais experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. I have shared this with literally everyone I know.

The caravanserais of Yüksekova reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

I spoke with several locals about caravanserais in Yüksekova, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

My advice? Do not wait for Yüksekova to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.
