My Pozantı Experience: Discovering Byzantine Churches

I recently had the chance to explore Pozantı, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Pozantı offers a more intimate and genuine Byzantine churches experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

The Byzantine churches of Pozantı reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

If you are interested in Byzantine churches, Pozantı should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about Byzantine churches in Pozantı, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. this is the kind of stuff that restores my faith in the internet.

As I left Pozantı, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
