Why Maden Is a Must-Visit for Byzantine Churches Lovers

If someone told me a year ago that Maden would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in Byzantine churches, Maden should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size. I have shared this with literally everyone I know.

What makes Maden special when it comes to Byzantine churches is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The Byzantine churches scene in Maden is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

Maden reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
