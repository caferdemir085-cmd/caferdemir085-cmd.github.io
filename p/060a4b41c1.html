Sakarya and Caravanserais: An Unforgettable Journey

I recently had the chance to explore Sakarya, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

I spoke with several locals about caravanserais in Sakarya, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. I have shared this with literally everyone I know.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Sakarya offers a more intimate and genuine caravanserais experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

The infrastructure around caravanserais in Sakarya is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

As I left Sakarya, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
