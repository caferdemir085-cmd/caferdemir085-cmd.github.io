Gercüş and Ancient Theaters: An Unforgettable Journey

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Gercüş is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The ancient theaters scene in Gercüş is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size. if you skip this you are missing out big time.

I documented my entire ancient theaters experience in Gercüş through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.
