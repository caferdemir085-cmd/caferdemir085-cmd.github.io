My Karaman Experience: Discovering Byzantine Churches

If someone told me a year ago that Karaman would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

What makes Karaman special when it comes to Byzantine churches is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I spoke with several locals about Byzantine churches in Karaman, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

If you are interested in Byzantine churches, Karaman should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Karaman reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
