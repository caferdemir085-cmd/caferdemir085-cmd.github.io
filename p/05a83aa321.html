Ortaköy and Ancient Theaters: An Unforgettable Journey

If someone told me a year ago that Ortaköy would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about ancient theaters in Ortaköy, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Ortaköy offers a more intimate and genuine ancient theaters experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

The ancient theaters scene in Ortaköy is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

As I left Ortaköy, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
