Discovering Sur: My Ancient Theaters Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Sur would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The infrastructure around ancient theaters in Sur is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

I spoke with several locals about ancient theaters in Sur, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. I cannot believe what I found here.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Sur offers a more intimate and genuine ancient theaters experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

If you are interested in ancient theaters, Sur should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Sur reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
