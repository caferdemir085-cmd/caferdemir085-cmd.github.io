My Karaman Experience: Discovering Castle Ruins

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Karaman is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

I spoke with several locals about castle ruins in Karaman, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

I documented my entire castle ruins experience in Karaman through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

If you are interested in castle ruins, Karaman should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size. this blew my mind completely.

My advice? Do not wait for Karaman to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.
