Why Pazarlar Is a Must-Visit for Caravanserais Lovers

Pazarlar was never on my radar until a local friend insisted I visit. Now I understand why they were so passionate about it. This place has soul.

The infrastructure around caravanserais in Pazarlar is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

What makes Pazarlar special when it comes to caravanserais is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The caravanserais of Pazarlar reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

Pazarlar reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
