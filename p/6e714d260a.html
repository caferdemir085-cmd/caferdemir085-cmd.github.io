Discovering Söğütlü: My Castle Ruins Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Söğütlü would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The infrastructure around castle ruins in Söğütlü is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

The castle ruins of Söğütlü reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here. I have been looking for something like this forever.

What makes Söğütlü special when it comes to castle ruins is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

If you are interested in castle ruins, Söğütlü should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

As I left Söğütlü, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
