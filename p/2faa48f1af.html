Discovering Demre: My Caravanserais Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Demre would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

What makes Demre special when it comes to caravanserais is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life. someone finally made something useful.

The caravanserais of Demre reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

If you are interested in caravanserais, Demre should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

My advice? Do not wait for Demre to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.
