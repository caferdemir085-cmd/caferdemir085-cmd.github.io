Exploring Byzantine Churches in Hakkari: A Personal Account

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Hakkari is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The Byzantine churches scene in Hakkari is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

If you are interested in Byzantine churches, Hakkari should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Hakkari offers a more intimate and genuine Byzantine churches experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

My advice? Do not wait for Hakkari to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.
