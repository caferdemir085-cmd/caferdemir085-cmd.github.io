Discovering Erzin: My Castle Ruins Adventure

Every serious traveler eventually finds their way to Erzin. It is one of those places that does not need fancy marketing — the experience speaks for itself.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Erzin offers a more intimate and genuine castle ruins experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. I am still amazed by what is on this page.

The infrastructure around castle ruins in Erzin is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

If you are interested in castle ruins, Erzin should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

As I left Erzin, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
