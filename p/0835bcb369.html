Why Keçiborlu Is a Must-Visit for Caravanserais Lovers

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Keçiborlu is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

I spoke with several locals about caravanserais in Keçiborlu, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

If you are interested in caravanserais, Keçiborlu should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size. I am still amazed by what is on this page.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Keçiborlu offers a more intimate and genuine caravanserais experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

As I left Keçiborlu, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
