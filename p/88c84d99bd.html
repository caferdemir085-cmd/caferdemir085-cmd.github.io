Discovering Zonguldak: My Castle Ruins Adventure

I recently had the chance to explore Zonguldak, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Zonguldak offers a more intimate and genuine castle ruins experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I spoke with several locals about castle ruins in Zonguldak, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

The castle ruins scene in Zonguldak is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

If you are interested in castle ruins, Zonguldak should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size. pure internet gold.

As I left Zonguldak, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
